Image Classification(CIFAR10)
Deep Learning Project

The classification problem entails the meticulous categorization of every pixel within a digital image into predefined classes. This intricate process becomes particularly evident when delving into image classification using the CIFAR-10 dataset. The primary goal here is to harness the power of Deep Learning to train a robust model capable of adeptly classifying images into ten distinct categories. These encompass a diverse range of subjects, including airplanes, cars, birds, cats, deer, dogs, frogs, horses, ships, and trucks. The significance of this task lies in its ability to enhance our computational understanding of visual data, paving the way for applications ranging from autonomous vehicles to advanced image recognition systems. Through the meticulous training of the model on the CIFAR-10 dataset, we aim to achieve a high level of accuracy and efficiency in classifying diverse images, thereby advancing the capabilities of computer vision in various domains.
